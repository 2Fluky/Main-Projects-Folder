Hanauma Bay

On the island of Oahu, Hanauma Bay is a protected bay that formed within the crater of a volcano. It is a popular destination for snorkeling and swimming due to its clear blue water and exotic marine life.



Volcanoes National Park

Volcanoes National Park covers over 300,000 acres of volcanoes, lava fields and more. The most notable aspect of the park is that it inludes the two most active volcanoes in the entire world.



Waimea Canyon State Park

Located on the west side of the island of Kauai, Waimea Canyon state park is over ten miles long and reaches depths of 3,600 feet. It is known for it's red rock cliffs, lush green foliage, and stunning waterfalls.



Na Pali Coast

Na Pali Coast is a state wilderness park that runs along the coast of Kaua'i. It is best known for its beautiful coastlines and towering cliffs (Some of which can reach up to 4,000 feet tall).



Hana Road

The Hana road is a highway located on the coast of Maui that connects Hana and Kahului. The highway passes through the island's tropical rainforests and natural landmarks, which makes for a stunning and scenic drive.



Maui Ocean Center

The Maui Ocean center is ranked among some of the best aquariums in the US. It exhibits the diverse ocean life of the hawaiian seas, which includes animals such as sharks, stingrays, jellyfish, sea turtles, and so much more.
